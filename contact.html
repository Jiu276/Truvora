Frequently Asked Questions

How to apply for product reviews?

You can contact us via email with detailed information about your product. We will evaluate based on factors such as product innovation and market demand.

Are reviews charged?

Our review service is free, but manufacturers need to provide test samples. We do not accept any form of paid reviews to ensure content objectivity.

How long does a review take?

Depending on product complexity, the review cycle is typically 1-4 weeks. We conduct thorough testing to ensure comprehensive and accurate reviews.

Can you request changes to review content?

Our reviews are based on objective testing and real experience, and we do not accept any modification requests. However, if factual errors are found, we will correct them promptly.
