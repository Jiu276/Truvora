Key Features

Built-in Ceramic Burr Grinder

The integrated ceramic burr grinder ensures fresh grounds for every cup. With 13 grind settings, you can customize the texture from fine espresso to coarser americano grinds. The ceramic burrs stay cooler than steel, preserving coffee's aromatic oils.

Beverage Variety

The Dinamica Plus can prepare 6 different beverages:

  • Espresso: Rich, concentrated shots with excellent crema
  • Americano: Long black coffee with customizable strength
  • Cappuccino: Perfect balance of espresso, steamed milk, and foam
  • Caffè Latte: Smooth milk coffee with minimal foam
  • Latte Macchiato: Layered drink with distinct coffee and milk layers
  • Hot Water: For tea or manual coffee preparation

TouchScreen Interface

The 3.5-inch color touchscreen makes operation intuitive. You can customize drink strength, temperature, and volume with simple taps. The interface is responsive and easy to navigate, even for first-time users.

Performance Testing

Coffee Quality

We tested the machine with premium beans over 30 days. Espresso shots consistently featured rich crema and balanced flavor. The temperature stability remained within 2°F of the target, ensuring consistent extraction.

Grinding Performance

The ceramic grinder produces uniform grounds across all settings. Fine settings work well for espresso, while coarser settings are perfect for americano. The grinder is louder than expected but completes grinding in 8-12 seconds.

Milk Frothing

The automatic milk frother creates dense, creamy foam for cappuccinos and lattes. While not as customizable as manual steam wands, it produces consistent results that satisfy most users.

Daily Use Experience

Setup and Installation

Initial setup takes about 15 minutes, including rinsing cycles and grinder calibration. The manual is clear and comprehensive, making the process straightforward.

Operation

Daily operation is simple: fill beans, add water, select your drink, and press start. The machine handles grinding, dosing, tamping, and brewing automatically. Most drinks are ready in 45-90 seconds.

Cleaning and Maintenance

The machine features automatic cleaning cycles for the coffee and milk systems. Daily maintenance involves emptying the drip tray and grounds container. Monthly descaling is required, and the machine prompts when needed.

Who Should Buy This Machine?

  • Households with multiple coffee drinkers

Long-term Value

At $799, the Dinamica Plus sits in the premium segment. However, consider the cost savings: cafe drinks average $4-6 each, so the machine pays for itself after 150-200 drinks. For daily coffee drinkers, this represents 2-3 months of use.

Final Recommendation

The DeLonghi Dinamica Plus excels at making cafe-quality coffee with minimal effort. While the initial investment is significant, the convenience, consistency, and variety make it worthwhile for serious coffee lovers.

If you're considering upgrading from a basic coffee maker or reducing cafe visits, this machine delivers excellent value and performance that will satisfy for years to come.
