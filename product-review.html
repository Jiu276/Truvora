Design & Build Quality

Sony has completely redesigned the WH-1000XM5 from the ground up, moving away from the foldable design of its predecessor. The new headphones feature a sleek, minimalist aesthetic with a lightweight construction that feels premium without being overly flashy.

The build quality is exceptional, with high-grade plastics and metal components that feel durable yet refined. The ear cups are spacious and well-padded, while the headband distributes weight evenly across your head for extended comfort sessions.

Standout Design Feature

The new stepless slider adjustment mechanism provides precise fit customization while maintaining the headphones' sleek profile.

Audio Performance

The WH-1000XM5 delivers Sony's most impressive audio performance yet. The 30mm drivers have been completely redesigned, offering a more balanced and natural sound signature compared to the bass-heavy approach of earlier models.

Vocals are crisp and detailed, instruments have excellent separation, and the bass response is tight and controlled without overwhelming other frequencies. The soundstage is impressively wide for closed-back headphones.

Noise Cancellation Technology

Sony's industry-leading noise cancellation has been further refined in the WH-1000XM5. The new V1 processor and dual noise sensor technology work together to virtually eliminate low-frequency ambient noise.

Noise Cancellation Performance

Airplane Cabin

Eliminates 95% of engine noise, making flights peaceful and comfortable.

Excellent reduction of train and bus noise, conversations remain slightly audible.

Effectively blocks HVAC systems and ambient office chatter.

Features & Smart Functionality

The WH-1000XM5 is packed with intelligent features that enhance the user experience. The Speak-to-Chat feature automatically pauses music when you start talking, while Adaptive Sound Control adjusts noise cancellation based on your activity and location.

Speak-to-Chat

Automatically detects when you're speaking and pauses music while reducing noise cancellation.

Adaptive Sound Control

Learns your daily activities and automatically adjusts ambient sound settings.

Quick Attention Mode

Cover the right ear cup to instantly lower volume and hear surroundings.

360 Reality Audio

Immersive 3D audio experience with compatible streaming services.

Battery Life & Connectivity

Sony claims 30 hours of battery life with ANC enabled, and our testing confirmed these figures under normal usage conditions. The quick charge feature provides 3 hours of playback with just 3 minutes of charging.
